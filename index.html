All Earthquake Data

Interactive visualization of earthquakes all over the world (in the past 7 days)

Below is a visualization of all the earthquakes in the last seven days. Data was collected from the United States Geological Survey(USGS). LeafletJS is used to plot the data. The data markers reflects the magnitude of the earthquake in their size and color e.g. earthquakes with higher magnitudes appears larger and darker in color. Popups provide additional information about the earthquake when a marker is clicked. A legend provides visual context of earthquake data.
